# Project Context & Guidelines

This file contains the fundamental guidelines for the `human-body` project.

Always consult and strictly adhere to the conventions and rules defined in the files within the `prompt/` folder for all your interactions and tasks:

- **`prompt/naming-conventions.md`**: Rules for naming classes, interfaces, methods, variables, etc.
- **`prompt/uml-conventions.md`**: Rules for UML diagrams and related structural concepts.
- **`prompt/unit-tests.md`**: Standards and rules for creating and maintaining unit tests.

**Mandate:**
Before writing code, creating tests, proposing architectural designs, or making structural decisions, you MUST read the files mentioned above to ensure your solution fully complies with the project's standards. These files take absolute precedence over general guidelines or defaults.
